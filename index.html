Checklist

Before trusting a parsed report

  • Confirm the report period is the period you expected.
  • Check every required field exists after extraction.
  • Compare row count with the normal monthly range.
  • Reconcile totals against the PDF and prior sheet output.

When to trigger human review

  • Layout version changed or labels were renamed.
  • Duplicate period detected in the destination sheet.
  • Month-over-month movement exceeds your threshold.
  • Extraction confidence drops below the accepted floor.
